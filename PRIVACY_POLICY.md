# Privacy Policy for Wedly

**Last updated: December 13, 2024**

Wedly ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Wedly mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you use the Wedly Application, we may collect the following personal information:
- Name and email address (for account creation)
- Profile pictures and service images
- Phone number
- Location data
- Wedding date and preferences
- Booking and transaction history

### Location Data
We collect location data to help you discover nearby wedding service providers (photographers, venues, caterers, decorators, makeup artists, etc.) based on your geographic location.

### Camera and Photo Access
We request camera and photo library access to allow you to:
- Upload profile pictures for your account
- Add service images if you are a service provider
- Share photos related to your wedding services or bookings

### Storage Access
We access your device storage to:
- Read and select photos from your gallery
- Store cached data for better app performance

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- **Service Provision**: To provide wedding marketplace features and connect users with service providers
- **Personalization**: To show relevant services based on your location and preferences
- **Communication**: To send booking confirmations, notifications, and updates
- **Analytics**: To improve our services and user experience
- **Payment Processing**: To facilitate transactions between users and service providers

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:
- **Service Providers**: Wedding vendors you book through the app
- **Third-Party Services**: For authentication (Google Sign-In, Facebook Login), payments, and analytics
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights

## Permissions Explanation

### Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)
**Why we need it**: Allows you to take photos directly within the app for profile pictures and service listings.

### Location Permissions (android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)
**Why we need it**: Enables location-based features to show nearby wedding service providers.

### Storage Permissions (android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_IMAGES)
**Why we need it**: Allows you to select photos from your device gallery to upload.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent for data processing
- Object to data processing

## Children's Privacy

Our Application is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
